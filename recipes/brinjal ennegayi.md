## Ingredients

### For Masala paste

- ¼ cup peanuts
- 2 tsp sesame seeds / til
- 2 tsp oil
- 2 tsp chana dal
- 1 tbsp coriander seeds
- 1 tsp  cumin / jeera
- ¼ tsp fenugreek seeds
- 10 dried red chilli
- few curry leaves
- ½ cup dry coconut / kopra (sliced)
- small ball sized tamarind
- 1 tbsp jaggery / gud
- ¼ tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ cup water

### other ingredients:

- 10 small brinjal / eggplant
- 3 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp mustard
- few curry leaves
- ½ cup water
